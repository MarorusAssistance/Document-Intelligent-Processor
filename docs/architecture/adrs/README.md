# Architecture Decision Records

ADRs document significant, non-obvious technical decisions made for this project.

## Template

```markdown
# ADR-NNNN — Title

**Status:** Accepted | Superseded by ADR-XXXX | Deprecated
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the problem or situation that led to this decision?
What constraints or requirements apply?

## Decision

What was decided?

## Consequences

What are the results of this decision?
Trade-offs: what becomes easier and what becomes harder?

## Alternatives considered

What other options were evaluated and why were they rejected?
```
